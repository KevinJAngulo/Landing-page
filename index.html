Who can see the my activity?

 Yes! You will be able to upload any song to the website and rate it so everyone can see what you think about it!

 We will run our website with ads if you decide to use the free version. You'll be able to rate a limited amount of songs until having to wait certain amount of time to resume your rating. If you decide to instead obtain the premium membership, the website will run completely add-free and your experience will be limitless in our environment. You'll be the first to be given updates and rewards as well as the ability to tests and review upcoming features!

 You should not need to worry about your privacy. Although our site runs ads (when free, check out Memberships), we do not accept or use third-party cookies to ensure that everything you do here with us, stays with us.

 The only people who can see your activity are moderators whose job is to matain a safe environment within our platform and whoever you give access to it, be it your friends or followers.
